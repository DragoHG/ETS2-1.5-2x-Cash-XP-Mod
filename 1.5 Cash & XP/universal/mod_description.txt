1.5 Cash & XP — Economy Mod for Euro Truck Simulator 2

A balanced boost: 1.5x job payouts and 1.5x experience (XP) gain.

Changes:
- Money multipliers: fixed_revenue, revenue_coef_per_km, cargo_market_revenue_coef_per_km, driver coefficients (1.5x)
- XP multipliers: delivery, free roam, road discovery, parking bonuses (1.5x)

IMPORTANT: Enable only ONE mod from the 2X Cash & XP family at a time.

Compatible with the latest ETS2 version and all map DLCs.
Does not modify fines, garage prices, or fuel costs.
