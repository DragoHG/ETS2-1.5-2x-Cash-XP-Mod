2X Cash & XP — Economy Mod for Euro Truck Simulator 2

Maximum boost: doubles both job payouts and experience (XP) gain.

Changes:
- Money (2.0x): fixed_revenue, revenue_coef_per_km, cargo_market_revenue_coef_per_km, driver coefficients
- XP (2.0x): delivery, free roam, road discovery, parking bonuses
- Hired drivers always return with cargo

IMPORTANT: Enable only ONE mod from the 2X Cash & XP family at a time.

Compatible with the latest ETS2 version and all map DLCs.
Does not modify fines, garage prices, or fuel costs.
