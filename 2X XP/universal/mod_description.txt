2X XP — Economy Mod for Euro Truck Simulator 2

Doubles experience (XP) gain from deliveries, free roam, road discovery, and manual parking bonuses. Job payouts remain at vanilla values.

Changes:
- exp_cargo_delivery, exp_free_roam, exp_road_discovery (2.0x)
- Manual parking bonuses — easy, medium, hard, double trailers, loading phase (2.0x)

IMPORTANT: Enable only ONE mod from the 2X Cash & XP family at a time.

Compatible with the latest ETS2 version and all map DLCs.
Does not modify fines, garage prices, or fuel costs.
