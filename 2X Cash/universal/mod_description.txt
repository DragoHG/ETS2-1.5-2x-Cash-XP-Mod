2X Cash — Economy Mod for Euro Truck Simulator 2

Doubles job payouts and driver revenue. Experience (XP) gain remains at vanilla values.

Changes:
- fixed_revenue, revenue_coef_per_km, cargo_market_revenue_coef_per_km (2.0x)
- Driver job revenue coefficients (2.0x)
- Hired drivers always return with cargo

IMPORTANT: Enable only ONE mod from the 2X Cash & XP family at a time.

Compatible with the latest ETS2 version and all map DLCs.
Does not modify fines, garage prices, or fuel costs.
